Gustav Klimt

Biography

Gustav Klimt was born on 14 July 1862 in Baumgarten, a part of today's 14th district in Vienna. He is considered the main figure of Austria’s Art Nouveau.

He was well known as a symbolism painter and one of the most prominent members of the Vienna Secession movement. .

What is special about him?

His pictures, painted with unobtrusive colours full of symbolic power, are a combination of two-dimensional, mosaic-like elements and Art Nouveau ornaments.
